Marathi Transliteration AI Training Dataset Guide

Objective

Convert Romanized Marathi (English letters) into correct Marathi
Devanagari script.

Examples: namaskar -> नमस्कार maharashtra -> महाराष्ट्र nashik -> नाशिक
shengadanyachi chikki -> शेंगदाण्याची चिक्की

Training Rules

1.  Convert pronunciation, not literal letters.
2.  Output only Marathi text.
3.  Do not translate meanings.
4.  Preserve punctuation and numbers.
5.  Support alternate spellings.
